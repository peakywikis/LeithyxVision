Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Welcome to Leithyx Vision. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Leithyx Vision collects data to provide AI-powered inventory tracking and cloud synchronization features:

  • Personal Info: User IDs (Firebase UID) and Email addresses (if you sign in with Google).
  • Photos: Images captured by your camera for object and text analysis.
  • Location: Approximate and Precise location data (if permitted) for contextual tagging of scans.
  • App Activity: Interaction data, such as which features you use and items you scan.
  • Performance Data: Crash logs and diagnostic info via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Device IDs: Unique identifiers for app instance management and security.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To analyze photos using ML Kit/AI and sync your history to your account via Firebase.
  • Analytics: To understand app performance and improve features.
  • Account Management: To maintain your profile and sync data across your devices.
  • Security: To prevent fraud and ensure the safety of our users.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party service providers to power Leithyx Vision:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, cloud database, and crash reporting.
  • Google ML Kit & Generative AI: For on-device and cloud-based image analysis.

These services may collect information sent by your device as part of their service delivery. You can review Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is stored as long as you have an account. You have full control over your data:

  • In-App Deletion: You can delete individual scans directly in the History tab.
  • Account Deletion: You can request full account and data deletion at any time.

For full instructions and links, visit our Data Deletion Page.

5. Security

All data transmitted from Leithyx Vision to our cloud services is encrypted in transit using Industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols. We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

6. Children's Privacy

Leithyx Vision is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
