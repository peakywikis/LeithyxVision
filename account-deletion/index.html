Leithyx Vision – Account & Data Deletion

Leithyx Vision is an AI-powered personal inventory and lifecycle tracker that uses on-device vision to scan and organize items, orders, and warranties.

How to Request Account Deletion

Leithyx Vision uses Firebase Authentication with anonymous guest sign-in. Users may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

Request via Email

Send an email to: peakywikis@zohomail.in

Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request – Leithyx Vision

Include the following information:

  • App name: Leithyx Vision
  • Approximate date of app usage
  • Optional details to help locate your data (no passwords or sensitive information required)

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Cloud-synced inventory data
  • Scanned item metadata
  • Warranty and order information
  • Anonymous Firebase Authentication identifiers

Data Retention

  • All user-associated data is deleted within 30 days
  • No personal identifiers such as email or phone number are stored
  • Aggregated, non-identifiable analytics data may be retained for legal or operational purposes

Account Creation Method

Leithyx Vision supports anonymous guest sign-in using Firebase Authentication, allowing users to use the app without creating a traditional account or providing personal credentials.

Contact

For questions regarding account or data deletion, contact: peakywikis@zohomail.in
