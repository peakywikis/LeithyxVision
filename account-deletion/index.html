Leithyx Vision: Data & Account Deletion

At Leithyx Vision, we value your privacy and give you full control over your data. Below are the steps to request the deletion of your account or specific data.

1. How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your scan history (including images, detected text, and notes) at any time directly within the app:

In-App Steps:
  1. Open the Leithyx Vision app.
  2. Navigate to the History tab from the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Select the item you wish to delete.
  4. Tap the Delete icon (Trash can). This will remove the data from your local device and our cloud sync.

To request a bulk deletion of all your scan data without deleting your account, please email us at:
peakywikis@zohomail.in

2. How to Delete Your Account

Deleting your account will permanently remove your user profile and all associated data from our servers.

To request account deletion:

Send an email to [YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL] with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Please include the email address or User ID associated with your account.

Request Account Deletion

3. What data is deleted?

  • Personal Identifiers: Your Firebase User ID and associated login metadata.
  • Scan Content: All images, OCR-detected text, category labels, and manual notes.
  • Lifecycle Data: Information regarding tracked orders, warranties, and return statuses.

Retention Period: Once a deletion request is processed, your data is removed from our active databases immediately. Backups may retain encrypted fragments for up to 30 days before being permanently overwritten.
